HISTORY

  1. 20/02/1967

    Birth of Kurt Cobain

    Kurt Cobain, the lead singer and guitarist of Nirvana, was born on February 20, 1967, in Aberdeen, Washington. He became one of the most influential figures in the grunge music scene.

  2. 1987

    Formation of Nirvana

    Nirvana was formed in 1987 by Kurt Cobain and bassist Krist Novoselic in Aberdeen, Washington. They experimented with different drummers before Dave Grohl joined in 1990.

  3. 1989

    Release of "Bleach"

    Nirvana released their debut studio album "Bleach" on June 15, 1989. The album showcased their raw, gritty sound and gained them a cult following.

  4. 24/09/1991

    Release of "Nevermind"

    Nirvana achieved mainstream success with "Nevermind," which included iconic tracks like "Smells Like Teen Spirit" and defined the grunge movement of the 1990s.

  5. 1992

    Marriage of Kurt Cobain and Courtney Love

    Kurt Cobain married Courtney Love on February 24, 1992. The couple had a daughter, Frances Bean Cobain, later that year.

  6. 05/04/1994

    Death of Kurt Cobain

    Kurt Cobain tragically passed away on April 5, 1994. His death marked the end of Nirvana, but the band's music continued to influence generations.

  7. 09/09/2011

    20th Anniversary of "Nevermind"

    A special re-release of "Nevermind" celebrated its 20th anniversary, featuring rare demos, B-sides, and live recordings.
